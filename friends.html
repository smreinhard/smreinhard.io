Friends

This section is about making friends in college.

Making friends in college can be difficult. However, use my advice below to make this process a little easier. I have been in college for two years, so I have valuable lessons to share on how to make friends. Below is a list of common places to make friends in college, some stories of how I made friends in college, and pictures of me and the friends I made!

Here are places where you can make friends in college.

  1. In a Class
  2. In Your Dorm
  3. At a School Event

Here are the stories of how I met my best three friends at college.:

Meeting Angela
Angela was a classmate of mine in my CHEM 109 discussion during my first semester of college. I became friends with Angela by going up to her at our first discussion session and saying "You look cool. We should be friends".
Meeting Julia
I met Julia because she lived in a dorm building right next to mine. I had heard of her through a mutual friend, and one day I decided to invite her to my dorm room to hang out. We have been friends since!
Meeting Sasha
I met Sasha because she was a friend of Julia's from high school. Once you make one good friend, you are bound to meet some of their friends and like them too!
